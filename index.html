Professional Narrative

I am a microbial genomics expert dedicated to combating Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR) through a One Health framework[cite: 3, 25, 271]. My research integrates advanced shotgun metagenomics and high-throughput sequencing to characterize multidrug-resistant pathogens and their transmission across human-animal interfaces.

Currently, I serve as the Chair of the ESCMID Study Group on Epidemiological Markers (ESGEM). My career includes pivotal roles at the University of Oxford, University of Groningen, and University of Bath, alongside leading international collaborations to standardize genomic surveillance.
